## Engagement Tips

1. **Respond to all comments within first hour** - Algorithm boost
2. **Use location tags** for major festivals (Coachella, EDC, Tomorrowland, etc.)
3. **Tag relevant accounts** - Festival pages, rave influencers, music artists
4. **Create a branded hashtag** - #PLMCommunity for user-generated content
5. **Encourage tagging** - Offer features/reposts for customers wearing PLM
6. **Go live** - Show products, answer questions, build hype
7. **Collaborate** - Partner with rave/festival influencers for authentic reach

## Visual Guidelines

- **Color Palette:** Use your brand colors (Yellow #F9F871, Red #EF4444, Green #10B981)
- **Filters:** Keep consistent - vibrant, high contrast, festival energy
- **Text Overlays:** Bold fonts, easy to read, match brand voice
- **Carousel Posts:** 3-5 images showing different angles/uses
