# Product Overview

Private Lives Matter (PLM) is an e-commerce storefront for a rave and festival clothing brand. The brand's mission: "To the peak, not the precinct."

## Core Value Proposition

Every item in the PLM™ Designs collection features a discreetly hand-sewn hidden pocket, allowing festival-goers to securely stash essentials and focus on the music and moment.

## Product Categories

- PLM™ Designs: Signature collection with hidden pockets (underwear, swimwear, activewear, accessories, headwear)
- Prints: Art prints and posters
- Second-Hand Gems: Curated vintage/second-hand items

## Key Features

- Dynamic shop filtering by brand, category, and collection
- Interactive product galleries with hover-to-preview
- Image magnification for detailed product inspection
- Persistent cart with localStorage
- Size/color selection with validation
- Responsive, mobile-first design

## Brand Voice

- Rebellious yet welcoming
- Festival/rave culture focused
- Emphasizes freedom, security, and community
- Tagline references avoiding security issues at festivals
